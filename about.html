ABOUT

Nashville based rockers, Flat Rock Revival have been bringing their tight, groove-oriented rock to the booty shakers of the Music City for over a decade. Blending 90’s Seattle tones, progressive rock roots, modern blues jam and electronic, psychedelic sounds, the band’s captivating live performances transport audiences to a salty, sweaty rock ‘n’ roll disco. Lyrically and vocally, the sound absolutely leans to the traditional folk themes of release, revolution and of course …… Revival.
